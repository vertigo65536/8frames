**8Frames bot Manual**

Command syntax is 8f(rames)!<game> <character> <command>
You can use 8f(rames)m(obile)!<game> <character> <command> for better mobile formatting
Valid games:
> t7
> sfv/sf5
> sf4
> sf3/3s

valid commands:
> punishable
> lose turn
> safe
> plus
> x(f) punish
> fireball/air/hit/fully invincible (sfv only)
> <move input>

If you need to send a buff with a command, send the command as:
> <buff>:<move input>
for example ```vt1:stlp```

use reactions to correct incorrect selections

characters must be sent as a single word. If the name is multiple names either use an acronym, send 1 word of the name, or remove the space.

examples of valid commands:

> 8frames!t7 dj df2
> 8frames!sfv bison punishable
> 8f!sfv chunli vt1:sthp
> 8f!sfv akuma vs1
> 8f!sf4 ryu hadoken
> 8f!3s yun 5f punish
